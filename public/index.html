Policies & FAQs

Are the first few classes free?

Yes. The first three live classes are offered as a free evaluation period. You can withdraw after the third session with no payment obligation.

How large is each batch?

We run small batches to protect quality: minimum 3 learners and maximum 6 learners.

Do you offer flexible payment plans?

Yes. We offer installment-based payment options. Payment terms are finalized after the free evaluation period.

What is your refund policy?

Simple and fair:

  • • Completed classes are non-refundable.
  • • Prepaid fees for future (unattended) classes can be refunded (pro-rata), based on a mutual written agreement.
  • • Alternative options: pause and resume in a future batch or transfer remaining value to another program (subject to availability).

Refunds apply only to unused portions of the program. Completed instructional sessions are not refundable. All refunds/pauses/transfers are finalized through a mutual written agreement.

What do I need to participate?

A laptop (Windows/macOS), stable internet, and willingness to practice labs.
